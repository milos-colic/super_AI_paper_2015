# Road to Super Artificial Intelligence

This repository contains a school paper I wrote more than 10 years ago on artificial intelligence, artificial emotions, self-awareness, and the path toward superintelligence.

The paper argues that intelligence is not complete if it includes only reasoning. Its central claim is that a truly intelligent system would need three things working together:

- cognitive intelligence
- emotional intelligence
- self-awareness

From there, it explores the idea that a system capable of modeling itself could eventually improve its own design, and that superintelligence would emerge through that kind of self-evolution rather than from a fixed human-built system.

This is an old academic paper, kept here as an early snapshot of how I was thinking about AI at the time.
